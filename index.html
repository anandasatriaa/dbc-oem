INTRODUCTION

PT. Milenia Mega Mandiri has been serving for 25 years in the automotive security systems and automotive electronics industry. We provide custom design solutions through joint development, manufacturing, assembly, and after-sales services for the automotive industry as well as for aftermarket customers through ODM business relationships.

PT. Milenia Mega Mandiri

With over 20 years of development experience, support from our technical partners in Taiwan & China, advanced equipment, and an experienced R&D team, we are always at the forefront of innovation in vehicle security systems and automotive electronics.

  • Founded: 1988
  • Founder: Mr. Charles Wong
  • Company Status: Private Ownership
  • Business Field: Automotive, Accessories Product & OEM Design Security System, ADAS
  • Operations: Cental Jakarta, South Jakarta, Cikupa, Tigaraksa Tangerang
  • Employees: More than 180

We are committed to delivering the best service with ISO 9001 and QE 9001 quality standards. Strategic collaborations with our partners and a deep understanding of customer needs have made us the primary choice in automotive security solutions.

Our Business

PT. Milenia Mega Mandiri operates across a diverse range of segments, including Retail, Project/OEM, and Wholesale. Our comprehensive approach ensures that we deliver innovative and reliable automotive security solutions to meet every market need.

Retail
